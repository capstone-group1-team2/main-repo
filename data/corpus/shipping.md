## How to Set Up a Shipping Address

No worries! I completely understand that you may not be familiar with the process of submitting a new delivery address. Allow me to walk you through the steps:

1. Begin by logging in to your account.
2. Look for the "My Account" or "Profile" section and click on it.
3. Within this section, search for "Shipping Addresses" or a similar option.
4. Click on it to access the page where you can add a new delivery address.
5. Fill in all the required fields, such as the recipient's name, address, and contact information.
6. Double-check your entries to ensure accuracy.
7. Once you're certain that all the details are correct, click on the "Submit" or "Save" button to finalize the process.
