# Cancel Policy & Support Guide


## Cancellation Fee Policy

Meridian Retail's cancellation fees depend on how far your order has progressed:

- **Within 1 hour of placing the order:** free cancellation, no fee, no questions asked.
- **After 1 hour but before the order has shipped:** free cancellation, processed within 1 business day.
- **After the order has shipped but before delivery:** a 10% restocking fee applies, deducted from your refund.
- **After delivery:** cancellation is no longer available through this process — use the standard 30-day return and refund process instead.

To view the exact cancellation status and any applicable fee for a specific order, log in to your online account portal and navigate to My Orders, then select the order and look for the Cancel Order option. The fee, if any, is shown before you confirm the cancellation.

I'll make it happen! To view the termination fee for a specific order, you can access My Orders in your account settings and select the order in question. The Cancel Order screen will show you the exact fee, if any, based on the order's current shipping status. If you have any further questions, feel free to let me know!

To check the early cancellation fee, you can log into your account on our website and navigate to My Orders. There you will find detailed information about any fees associated with cancelling your order before it's delivered, based on the timing tiers above.
