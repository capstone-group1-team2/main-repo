You can reach out to our customer support team via phone at 1-800-555-0199 or email at support@meridianretail.com. One of our representatives will be more than happy to assist you with submitting your feedback.

Alternatively, you can also visit our website at www.meridianretail.com and navigate to the "Contact Us" section. There, you'll find a feedback form where you can provide detailed information about your experience and submit it directly to us. Rest assured, all feedback received is carefully reviewed and shared with the relevant departments for further action.
